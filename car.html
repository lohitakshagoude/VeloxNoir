Help & Guide

  • Browsing Brands: The main grid on the homepage displays all ten luxury car brands. Simply hover over any brand card to see a zoom animation, then click on it to enter that brand's dedicated page.
  • Search Bar: Use the gold search bar in the top-right of the navigation to filter brands by name. As you type, only matching brand cards will be shown — all others disappear. Clear the search to restore all cards.
  • Navigation Links: The top navigation bar has four links — Home takes you back to the top, Help scrolls to this section, Settings takes you to preferences, and Assistance gives you direct official links to every brand.
  • Do NOT refresh mid-browsing — if you are deep inside a brand's page, use your browser's back button to return to this main page safely.
  • Image not loading? If a car image shows as broken, the image file may be missing from the project folder. Make sure all .png image files are placed in the same directory as this HTML file.
  • Clicking brand cards: Each card is fully clickable — you can click anywhere on the card (image or text) to open that brand's page.
  • Smooth scrolling: All navigation links scroll smoothly to their sections. You can also manually scroll down to reach Help, Settings, and Assistance.
